# Domain Purchase — chase.md

## TL;DR

`.md` is Moldova's ccTLD. Expect **~$50-60/yr** registration. Cheapest registrars are around **$49/yr**. Renewals can be higher at some registrars — check renewal price before buying.

## Cheapest Registrars (as of Feb 2026)

| Registrar | Registration | Renewal | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|---------|-------|
| **nic.md** (official registry) | ~$39/yr | ~$39/yr | Direct from Moldovan registry. Cheapest but basic UI |

## Recommendation

1. **First choice: Porkbun or Spaceship** — cheapest reputable registrars with transparent renewal pricing (~$50/yr)
2. **If you want direct:** nic.md — cheapest at $39/yr but less polished
3. **Avoid:** Gandi, Domain.com, GoDaddy — all charge 3-5x the market rate for .md

## Availability

chase.md needs to be checked at purchase time — `.md` domains don't always show up correctly in WHOIS tools. Best to search directly on:
- https://www.porkbun.com/checkout/search?q=chase.md
- https://spaceship.com (search "chase.md")
- https://nic.md

**Risk:** It's a short, dictionary-word domain. May already be taken or parked. If unavailable, consider:
- **chasedocs.md**
- **getchase.md**
- **usechase.md**

## .md Domain Notes

- **Registry:** MoldData (nic.md)
- **No residency requirement** — anyone can register
- **DNSSEC:** Supported
- **Transfer:** Standard EPP transfer, 60-day lock after registration
- **Fun fact:** .md is also used for Markdown associations (deno.md, docs.md, etc.) which works nicely for a tech product
